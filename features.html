Fly Buddy Features

Voice-Activated Emergency Assistance

Fly Buddy provides instant, spoken guidance in critical situations. Simply say, “Mayday, engine failure”, and Fly Buddy will:

  • Recognize the emergency immediately
  • Give clear, step-by-step voice instructions
  • Suggest the nearest landing site
  • Guide pilots through the safest recovery procedures

AI-Powered Situational Awareness

Fly Buddy monitors real-time conditions to prevent incidents before they happen:

  • Detects weather risks, fuel concerns, and airspace restrictions
  • Alerts pilots to low visibility, turbulence, or unsafe approach paths
  • Provides diversion recommendations and alternate landing sites

Smart Flight Planning & Decision Support

Beyond emergencies, Fly Buddy acts as an AI co-pilot, assisting with:

  • Optimal route planning with live weather and NOTAM updates
  • Fuel efficiency calculations and alternate airport suggestions
  • Turbulence and storm avoidance for the smoothest flight experience

Stress Reduction & AI-Powered Focus

Fly Buddy helps pilots stay calm and focused under pressure.

  • Conversational AI that provides clear, human-like guidance
  • Reduces cognitive overload by simplifying complex decisions
  • Acts as a non-panic AI Copilot, ensuring pilots always have a second set of eyes
